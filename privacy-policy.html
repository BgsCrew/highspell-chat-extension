This privacy policy describes how the Highspell Chat Extension ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. The extension only stores your preferences locally in your browser, including:

  • Chat color settings
  • Message visibility preferences (which chat types to hide)
  • Timestamp display options

This data remains on your device and is synchronized through your browser's built-in sync feature only if you choose to enable it in your browser settings.

How We Use Your Information

The locally stored preferences are used solely to customize your chat experience on Highspell.com. We do not access, analyze, or share this information with any third parties. The extension does not track your browsing behavior, collect analytics, or monitor your gameplay activities.

Data Storage and Security

All settings are stored using your browser's secure storage API (browser.storage.sync). If you have browser sync enabled in your browser settings, these preferences may sync across your devices through your browser provider's encrypted sync service (Google Chrome, Firefox, etc.). We do not have access to this sync process or the synced data.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The extension does not make network requests to any external servers. The only interaction is with Highspell.com itself, where the extension modifies the chat interface as intended.

Data Retention

Your preferences are stored locally until you either uninstall the extension or manually clear your browser's extension data. We do not retain any data on external servers.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. You can:

  • View and modify your settings through the extension's popup and settings pages
  • Clear all extension data by uninstalling the extension
  • Reset settings to defaults through the extension interface

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above and distributed with extension updates through the browser's extension store.
